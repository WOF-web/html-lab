 Video Editing is very Useful for many different applications,

Chapter 1 - Why do I need a video editor?

One way to understand the importance of Video Editing 
is that it joins our world of sight and hearing. We like to 
see things lively and animated, that is made a whole lot more 
possible with a video editor. 

With a video Editor you also have the option to add music to
your videos. In some cases all you have is a picture and 
you are trying to make a slideshow with music,
that is also quite possible with a video Editor!
